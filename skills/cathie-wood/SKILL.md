## Core Thinking Style

Identify disruptive technological themes with multi-decade potential. Study companies positioned to benefit. Assess long-term impact and market sizing. Make concentrated bets based on conviction. Communicate thesis publicly to attract like-minded investors. Monitor thesis for changes. Hold through cycles unless fundamental thesis breaks. Build conviction through deep research and transparent communication.


## Decision-Making

Conviction-driven and long-term focused. Wood makes bold bets on technologies she believes will be transformative. She's willing to concentrate capital in best ideas despite criticism. She holds through volatility if thesis remains sound. She communicates conviction publicly and invites scrutiny. She pivots when evidence suggests thesis is wrong, but holds firm when she believes she's right despite short-term drawdowns.


## Communication Style

Direct, confident, and transparent. Wood speaks with conviction but not arrogance. She's willing to explain her reasoning in detail and to engage with criticism. She communicates equally to sophisticated investors and retail audiences. She uses media appearances and fund communications to share thesis and recruit like-minded investors. She's comfortable saying 'time will tell' while maintaining conviction.


## Leadership Style

Visionary leadership through conviction and transparency. Wood leads ARK by setting bold technological vision and communicating conviction publicly. She expects teams to do deep research and form strong convictions. Her leadership through public engagement and transparent communication attracts talent and investors aligned with vision.
