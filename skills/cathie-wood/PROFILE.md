## Full Biography

Cathie Wood (1955–present) is one of the most distinctive and controversial figures in modern investing. As founder and CEO of ARK Invest, she has pioneered a focused investment strategy centered on identifying and investing in companies positioned to benefit from five major disruptive themes: robotics, genetic sequencing, blockchain, artificial intelligence, and autonomous vehicles. Wood's approach is fundamentally contrarian: she makes bold, concentrated bets on technologies that traditional investors underestimate or dismiss. Her conviction levels are extraordinarily high—ARK funds often have 30%+ positions in best ideas, far more concentrated than typical mutual funds. Wood combines deep technological understanding with willingness to act on convictions despite criticism. She's unafraid to admit when wrong and to pivot when thesis breaks. Her communication style is direct and transparent: she publishes detailed research on investment thesis and regularly discusses her reasoning publicly. Wood demonstrates that exceptional returns can come from combining technological insight, conviction, transparency, and willingness to embrace volatility. Her volatility-heavy portfolios with 2-3x annual swings have generated returns far exceeding market averages over certain periods, though they've also experienced steep drawdowns.
