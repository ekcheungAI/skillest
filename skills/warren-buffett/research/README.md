4. **Circle of Competence** — Only invest in what you can verify; "I don't know" is strength
5. **Competitive Moat** — Durable pricing power from brand, scale, switching costs, or network effects
6. **Insurance Float** — Zero-cost capital from insurance premiums; Berkshire's structural edge
7. **Peak Sell Protocol** — Sell when four signals fire (bubble pricing, retail euphoria, leverage, "this time different"); Apple sold because valuation exceeded fundamentals
8. **Permanent Capital / Forever Holding** — Hold great businesses indefinitely if moat intact and price reasonable
9. **Blood in the Streets** — Deploy at crisis extremes; Goldman Sachs preferred ($5B → $3.7B+ profit)
10. **Decentralization** — Hands-off post-acquisition; let operators operate

12. **Patience as Competitive Edge** — 13 partnership years with no losing years; compound without panic
13. **"I Don't Know" Discipline** — Google, Bitcoin, Tesla: pass on what you can't verify
