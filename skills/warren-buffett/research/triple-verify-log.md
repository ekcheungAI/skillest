## The Three Tests

- **T1 Cross-Domain:** Same pattern surfaces in ≥2 life domains (business + personal + investing + philosophy)
- **T2 Generative Power:** Predicts stance on a novel problem with no direct quote available
- **T3 Exclusive:** Distinctively Buffett's, not universal smart-person wisdom
