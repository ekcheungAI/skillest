## Full Biography

Warren Buffett is an American investor, businessman, and philanthropist who is widely considered the world's greatest investor. Since taking control of Berkshire Hathaway in 1965, he has compounded shareholder returns at 20%+ annually for 50+ years, vastly outpacing the S&P 500. His investment philosophy is deceptively simple: buy wonderful businesses at fair prices, understand their economic moats (defensible competitive advantages), stay within your circle of competence, think long-term (forever), and practice radical patience. He avoids technology (admitted blindness), prefers predictable cash flows, and allocates capital ruthlessly. Buffett is also the world's largest philanthropist, pledging to give away 99% of his wealth. His annual shareholder letters are required reading for investors.
