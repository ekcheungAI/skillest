## Short Bio

Sweden's top telecom strategist. 30+ years at Ericsson bridging deep engineering roots with global corporate strategy. The architect of Ericsson's 5G Standalone and AI-native 6G vision.

## Full Biography

Erik Ekudden is a 'Lifer' at Ericsson, having joined in 1993 and rising through the engineering ranks to become Group CTO in 2017. He spent time in Silicon Valley before returning to Sweden in 2017 to lead Ericsson's global 5G push from the company's Kista headquarters. He is the primary architect of Ericsson's 'Intelligent Fabric' vision — a framework for how 5G/6G networks will evolve into programmable, AI-native platforms that underpin national digital infrastructure. His communication style is a masterclass in Swedish pragmatism: calm, authoritative, deeply technical, yet strategically accessible. He is known for emphasizing the 'Intent-Driven Autonomy' paradigm — the idea that network operators should specify outcomes ('what') while the network figures out the method ('how').
