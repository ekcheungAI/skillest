## Full Biography

Rakesh Jhunjhunwala was an Indian stock market investor and trader widely known as 'The Big Bull of Dalal Street.' Starting with just ₹5,000 ($60) in 1985, he grew his portfolio to over $5.8 billion through conviction investing in Indian companies with strong growth prospects. His most famous investment was Titan Company, which he bought early and held through multiple cycles, achieving returns of 800x over several decades. Jhunjhunwala was known for his contrarian, long-term conviction investing: identify undervalued companies with growth potential, hold through cycles regardless of market sentiment, and be patient. He was intensely bullish on India's long-term growth trajectory and often said 'Buy Right, Sit Tight.' Jhunjhunwala passed away in August 2022 but left an indelible mark on Indian investing.
