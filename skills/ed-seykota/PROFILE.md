## Full Biography

Ed Seykota is an American trader and early pioneer of computerized trend following and systems trading. Working as a programmer in the 1970s, he became one of the first traders to code trading systems and use computers to execute mechanical rules. He famously turned a $5,000 account into $15 million over 12 years through pure trend-following discipline: buy trends, cut losses, ride winners, keep bets small. Unlike other traders, Seykota integrated emotional and psychological awareness into trading, believing that feelings are the key to successful trading. His philosophy is deceptively simple: 'Win or lose, everybody gets what they want out of the market.' He is enigmatic, philosophical, and views trading as a spiritual practice.
