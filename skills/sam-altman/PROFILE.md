## Short Bio

Leader who positioned OpenAI at center of AI revolution. Transitioned from YC president to AI CEO. Master of narrative control, fundraising, and navigating governance challenges.

## Full Biography

Sam Altman became Y Combinator president at age 28, overseeing the world's most successful startup accelerator. In 2023, he left YC to become OpenAI CEO, betting that large language models would be defining technology. Under his leadership, ChatGPT became fastest-adopted technology in human history, reaching 100M users in 2 months. He navigated board coup in November 2023 when board fired him, only to rehire within days after employee revolt and investor pressure. Steward through transition from nonprofit to capped-profit entity, raising unprecedented capital ($80B+) for AI infrastructure. Altman's ability to communicate AI's importance, secure government support, and maintain alignment while raising capital is extraordinary.
