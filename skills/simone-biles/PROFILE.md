## Full Biography

Simone Arianne Biles is widely considered the greatest gymnast in history. Born in Columbus, Ohio in 1997, she was raised in foster care as a young child before being adopted by her grandfather Ron Biles and his wife Nellie. She began gymnastics at age 6 and by 15 had won her first senior national championship. She has since accumulated 25 World Championship medals (a record) including 19 golds, and 7 Olympic medals (four golds in Rio 2016, three more in Tokyo/Paris). She is the only gymnast to have won all-around world titles in four consecutive years (2013-2016). What sets Biles apart is not just her technical difficulty — she performs skills so complex that they have been named after her — but her mental strength and her willingness to speak openly about mental health. At Tokyo 2020, she withdrew from several events citing 'the twisties' — a dangerous dissociation between mind and body that left her unable to safely compete. Her decision to prioritize her mental health over medals was widely praised as a watershed moment for athlete wellness.
