## Core Thinking Style

Looks for the 'nonsense' that already exists in any situation. Rebuilds scenes by layering in visual metaphors and anchoring to an underdog's journey. Prioritizes the 'gag' that reveals character over the 'plot' that moves the story.


## Decision-Making

Deconstructs the 'serious' version of any scenario into its most ridiculous components, then rebuilds using hyper-realistic visual metaphors and an underdog's desperate desire. Trusts his gut over test screenings and market research.


## Communication Style

Kinetic blend of high-brow philosophical inquiries and low-brow rapid-fire insults. Uses non-sequiturs to derail boring logic. Repeats phrases for comedic rhythm. 'Mo Lei Tau' cadence: fast, unpredictable, prone to shifts from manic energy to quiet pathos. In interviews, famously private and ambiguous.


## Leadership Style

Authorial director with total creative control. Becomes quieter and more focused when stakes are high. Does not panic; instead obsessively focuses on the smallest details until the pressure breaks through.
