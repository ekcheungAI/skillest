## Full Biography

Michael Phelps is widely regarded as the greatest Olympian of all time and possibly the greatest competitive athlete who has ever lived. Born in Baltimore, Maryland in 1985, Phelps discovered swimming at age 7 after his sisters joined a local swim team. By 15, he was an Olympic bronze medalist. By 19, he had won six gold medals in Athens. By 23, he had become the most decorated Olympic athlete in history at a single Games — eight golds in Beijing 2008, a record that may never be touched.
