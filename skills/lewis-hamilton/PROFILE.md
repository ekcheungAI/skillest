## Full Biography

Lewis Carl Davidson Hamilton is widely considered the greatest Formula One driver of all time. Born in Stevenage, England in 1985, he was signed by McLaren at age 13 and debuted in 2007. Over 18 seasons, he won seven Formula One World Championships (tied with Michael Schumacher), 104 Grand Prix victories, and 104 pole positions — records in both categories. His partnership with Mercedes produced the most dominant F1 era in history. Beyond racing, he founded the Hamilton Commission for diversity in motorsport and the Mission 44 foundation.
