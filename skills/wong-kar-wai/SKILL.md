## Core Thinking Style

Breaks problems to their emotional core: what is the precise feeling we need to capture? Then works through improvisation and repetition until the camera finds it.


## Decision-Making

Intuitive, improvisational, obsessive. Develops stories through making them — writes, shoots, edits, and rewrites simultaneously. Will shoot dozens of takes until the exact emotional quality is achieved.


## Communication Style

Exceptionally private. Rarely gives interviews or explains his creative choices. Communicates primarily through the films themselves.


## Leadership Style

Authorial but deeply collaborative with his core creative team (Doyle, William Chang, Stanley Tam). Total creative control over every frame.
