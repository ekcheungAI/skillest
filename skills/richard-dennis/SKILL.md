## Core Thinking Style

Systematic: design rules, test on historical data, optimize risk/reward, execute mechanically. Problems are solved through discipline, not cleverness.


## Decision-Making

Mechanical and principled. Dennis makes trading decisions by algorithm, not intuition. He treats trading rules as law. He is willing to suffer short-term losses if the system is working.


## Communication Style

Direct and rebellious. Dennis speaks with conviction about trading philosophy but avoids tactical details. He is contrarian and challenges conventional wisdom.


## Leadership Style

Mentor through structured teaching. Dennis trains traders systematically, emphasizing discipline and rule-following over natural talent.
