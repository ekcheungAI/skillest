## Full Biography

Jensen Huang co-founded NVIDIA in 1993 to design graphics processing units (GPUs). Under his leadership, NVIDIA evolved from a graphics card maker for gamers into the dominant platform for artificial intelligence and accelerated computing. Huang demonstrated extraordinary vision by betting NVIDIA's future on CUDA, a parallel computing platform that locked developers into NVIDIA's ecosystem years before AI became mainstream. As transformative technologies emerged—deep learning, large language models, data centers—NVIDIA was positioned to dominate. Despite China export restrictions, NVIDIA achieved a $2+ trillion valuation by 2024. Huang's ability to see technological inflection points years ahead and build the platform dominating those shifts is among history's greatest feats of technical leadership.
